Emphasis & Importance

Bold/important text uses <strong>.
Visually bold (no extra meaning) uses <b>.
Emphasized/italic text uses <em>.
Visually italic (no extra meaning) uses <i>.

Marked, Small, Deleted, Inserted

Highlighted text with <mark>.
Small print / disclaimer with <small>.
Deleted/struck-through text with <del>.
Inserted/underlined text with <ins>.

Subscript & Superscript

Water is H2O.

Einstein: E = mc2.
